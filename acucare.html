AI-powered mobile app for acne detection, classification, and personalized treatment recommendations using YOLOv8, ELM ensemble models, and Flutter.

AcuCare is an AI-powered mobile application designed to assist users in identifying acne types and receiving personalized treatment recommendations. The app combines computer vision (YOLOv8 acne detection) with machine learning (ELM-based classification) to accurately recognize acne categories (nodule, papule, pustule). It also suggests over-the-counter treatments, skin care routines, and dermatology products, making acne management more accessible.

Acne is one of the most common skin conditions worldwide, yet many people struggle to correctly identify its type. Misclassification often leads to ineffective treatments, prolonged breakouts, and wasted money on unsuitable products. Dermatologist consultations are expensive and not always accessible, especially in developing regions. There was a need for a fast, affordable, and user-friendly solution that could guide users toward the right treatment path.

AcuCare empowers users to take control of their skin health by providing instant, AI-driven acne analysis through their smartphones. By identifying acne types and offering personalized treatment guidance, the app reduces reliance on guesswork and helps prevent misdiagnosis. It not only improves treatment accuracy but also makes acne care affordable and accessible, bridging the gap between dermatologists and everyday users.

Key Features

  • Real-time Acne Detection & Classification
  • Survey Analysis
  • Treatment Recommendations
  • Interactive UI
  • Manual Cropping
  • Results Visualization
