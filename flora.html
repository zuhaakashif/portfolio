Gardening Community Website

https://github.com/zuhaakashif/Gardening-Website-UI

A community-driven gardening website UI that connects urban farmers and gardeners, helping them track their activities, share resources, and collaborate through forums, events, and projects.

The Gardening Community Website is designed to bring together gardening enthusiasts into one platform. It allows users to log their contributions, participate in community discussions, access educational resources, showcase projects, and even shop for gardening toolsâ€”all in an engaging, modern interface.

Many gardening enthusiasts lack a centralized digital space to track activities, share resources, and collaborate with like-minded people. Existing platforms are fragmented, making it harder for communities to grow and stay engaged.

By providing a centralized platform, this UI fosters stronger community involvement, encourages sustainable gardening practices, and empowers users with resources and tools to enhance their gardening journey.
